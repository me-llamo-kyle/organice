** Monoids central to category theory
Associative product + unit
A category can be viewed as a generalized monoid:
- Unit = identity morphisms O -id-> A
- Product = pullback of domain and codomain functions A =dom/cod=> O
- A X_O A = {(g,f) in A X A: dom(g) = cod(f)}
** Categorical product is associative, symmetric, and has terminal objects as units
(All up to isomorphism)
