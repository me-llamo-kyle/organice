#+title: Weekly Review

* Steps
** Make sure each project has at least one next action
** Add events to calendar iff they MUST be done on/by that date - hard deadlines only
** Make sure each next action is actually something you want or need to do if you have time during the coming week - otherwise move to Someday or delete
** Look through Someday list to see if anything should be moved to Projects/Actions

Reminders of any "open loops" that may not have been captured yet
